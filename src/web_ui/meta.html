🔍 Search Filter Rules

Text / Sequence Header Columns:
  • Substring Match: Matches anywhere in the text by default (e.g. candidatus).
  • Wildcard Match: Use * for zero or more characters, or ? for a single character (e.g. *coli*, b*subtilis, or node?).
Numerical Columns (e.g. Length):
  • Range: Format min-max (e.g. 100-300).
  • Operators: Prepend with math operators:
    • >=100 (greater than or equal to 100)
    • <=150 (less than or equal to 150)
    • >50 (greater than 50)
    • <200 (less than 200)
    • !=10 (not equal to 10)
    • =10 or just 10 (exactly equal to 10)
  • Logical Commands: Combine comparisons enclosed in matching parentheses with operators:
    • & (AND): e.g. (>=100)&(<=500)
    • | (OR): e.g. (>10)|(<3)
    • ^ (XOR): e.g. (>100)^(>200)

🖱️ Interaction Rules

  • Network Selection Filtering: Selecting a group of nodes in the network viewer (e.g., using the selection tool) will automatically filter the spreadsheet to display only the data for those specific nodes.
  • Node Click Highlighting: Left-clicking an individual node in the network viewer will automatically locate, highlight, and scroll to its corresponding entry in the spreadsheet.
